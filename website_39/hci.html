HCI

Design Principles

Considering our core users are blind people, we made attempts to identify blind users to interview. After researching this issue we discovered that it will not be possible for us to find blind users (we confirmed this with Dave Twisleton). The solution will be for us to interview classmates in our related Teams (Team 40 members and Team 41 members).

To demonstrate the persona and scenario, we sketched a storyboard and made physical prototype constructed of foam board. We also tried out the Soundscape application and experimented with it, as well as with the Cortana application.
